About Us

Subtitle

Here at Take It Cheesy, we pride ourselves in making ethical and environmentally sourced cheeses from pure, wholesome organic milk. Unlike larger scale industrialized cheese producers, we focus on the quality and craft of producing the most quality artisanal cheeses. The bigger cheese producers focus only on trying to produce at a wider scale by altering and adjusting the composition of the milks and proteins used in cheesemaking. We feel this takes away from the unique flavour notes and textures that we feel makes cheese so amazing. Our focus lies in our handcrafted process of turning high quality milk from a happy and well treated cow, which is then transformed into cheese of unique tastes and textures that end up revealing the subtle range of flavours influenced by seasons, forage and feed. Each wheel of cheese is produced by hand, ensuring genuine care and quality, but also resulting in cheeses that are robust and complex in taste.
